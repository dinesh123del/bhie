# Questions for Speech-to-Action Integration

1. **Automation Level**: Should actions like "Add Expense" be performed **automatically** (hands-free), or should they stay in a "Pending" state for you to confirm manually?
2. **Feedback Mode**: Do you want a **Voice response** (Text-to-Speech) back from the app ("Action completed, sir"), or just a **Visual notification** in the HUD?
3. **Trigger keywords**: Are there any specific phrases you want me to prioritize for triggering actions (e.g., "AERA, record this...")?
